GDPR Compliance Toolkit

An open source toolkit for developers building GDPR-compliant applications. Covers Article 30 record-keeping, data subject rights, consent management, and audit logging.

Features

  • Automated Article 30 processing records
  • Data subject request handling
  • Consent management framework
  • Access audit logging
  • Data breach notification templates
